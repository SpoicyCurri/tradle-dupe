How to Play

Guess the mystery country based on its export data.

  • Each guess must be a valid country name
  • After each guess, you'll receive information about:
  • - Distance: How far your guess is from the target country
  • - Direction: Which direction to go from your guess
  • - Population: Whether the target has a larger or smaller population
  • - GDP: Whether the target has a higher or lower GDP

A new puzzle is available each day!
